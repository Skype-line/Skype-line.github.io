Summary

We contribute:
The first expressive implicit human avatar model that captures body pose, hand pose, facial expressions and appearance in a holistic fashion.
Part-aware initialization and sampling strategies, which together improve the quality of the results and keep training efficient.
A new dataset consisting of 234 sequences, of high-quality textured scans showing 20 participants with varied body and hand movements, and facial expressions, totalling 35,000 frames.
