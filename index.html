Kaiyue Shen

shenkaiyue1997@gmail.com
I’m currently a Deep Learning & Computer Vision Engineer at Arbrea Labs AG, Switzerland. I earned my BSc in Electronic Information Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (2019), where I completed my bachelor’s thesis under Prof. Yang Hu on Virtual Try-on using Generative Adversarial Networks. I then obtained my MSc in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at ETH Zürich (2022). My master’s thesis, X-Avatar: Expressive Human Avatars, was supervised by Prof. Otmar Hilliges and accepted at CVPR 2023.
Between September 2022 and March 2023, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Advanced Interactive Technologies Lab (AIT), ETH Zürich, focusing on 3D digital human animation and human pose estimation. Prior to that, I was a Computer Vision Intern at Seervision AG, Zürich (September 2021 – March 2022).
My work focuses on digital humans and spans 2D and 3D computer vision, including reconstruction, animation, and modeling, with experience in both research and product development.
Outside of work, I’m an explorer at heart and enjoy skiing, tennis, badminton, fitness, hiking, cycling, ice skating, and baking.
